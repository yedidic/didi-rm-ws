About Didi

Presence, curiosity and a human meeting

I’m Didi Cohen, a Kundalini Yoga teacher and teacher trainer based in Jerusalem.

I teach a weekly group at Beit Shel Yoga and offer private classes shaped around your pace and needs.

Together with Maayan, I care for Beit Shel Yoga—a Jerusalem studio and community offering space for practice and connection. My background also includes a BSW from Bar-Ilan University, studies in Rosen Method Bodywork and work as a software developer; these fields deepen the way I listen to the body, the person, complexity and each practitioner’s individual pace.
